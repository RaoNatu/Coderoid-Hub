How to Create a Standout LinkedIn Profile

by Kritika Bhattarai

August 05, 2024

Ready to transform your LinkedIn profile from “Mehhh” to “Woahh”?

Let’s dive in and make your profile pop-up with personality and professionalism!

Step 1: Setting Up Your Profile Picture

Profile Picture

  • Choose a professional but friendly photo. Look approachable, confident, and ready to conquer the professional world.

    • Show off that winning smile!!

Step 2: Crafting Your Top Card

Headline

  • You’ve got 220 characters to grab attention. Make them count!

Try something like, “Marketing Wizard | Turning Ideas into Gold” or “Tech Enthusiast | Bridging Innovation and Impact.”

Contact Information

  • Add your business email and phone number. Make it super easy for people to get in touch with you.

    • You’ll get a lot more DMs than you expect ;P

Featured Section

  • If you love creating content, highlight your best posts, articles, or projects here.

    • Let your work speak for itself!

Step 3: The About Section – Your Story, Your Way

  • You have up to 2,600 characters. Use them to tell your story. Highlight your journey, passions, and what you bring to the table. Make it engaging and personal. Think of it as your elevator pitch – but way cooler and way more impactful.

Step 4: Experience – Highlight Your Growth

  • List your job titles and include full company names and dates of employment. Use strong action verbs and showcase your achievements. “Increased sales by 30%” sounds way better than “responsible for sales”.

Step 5: Education – Relevant and Recent

  • Include your educational background as far back as it’s relevant to your current career. Don’t forget to highlight any special academic projects or honors.

Step 6: Licenses & Certifications – To Boost Your Credibility

  • Add any professional licenses and certifications that make you stand out.

    • Featured Section

Step 7: Skills – Show Off Your Expertise

  • You can list up to 50 skills but make sure they’re relevant and highlight your strengths.

Step 8: Recommendations – Social Proof

  • Collect recommendations from colleagues, mentors, and clients. They add credibility and make you look even more awesome.

Step 9: Publications, Courses, and Organizations

  • If you’ve published articles, taken relevant courses, or belong to professional organizations, list them here. They add depth and breadth to your profile.

Step 10: Keep It Fresh – Regular Updates

  • Regularly review and update your LinkedIn profile. Keep it current and relevant. Adjust your keywords and phrases to stay aligned with search trends.

The Fun Stuff: Making It Engaging

  • Be Yourself
    • Use your own words and tone. If your audience appreciates humor, don’t shy away from it. Let your personality shine!
  • Tell Stories
    • Use your About section to share personal anecdotes and stories that highlight your professional journey. Everyone loves a good story.
  • Add Multimedia
    • Spice up your profile with photos, videos, and presentations. Make it dynamic and visually appealing.
  • Engage with Your Audience
    • If someone messages you on LinkedIn, ask them how they found you and what keywords they used. This can help you refine your profile even further.
